Maicrest Mobile App Privacy Policy

App Description

Maicrest is a privacy-focused mobile application designed to help users control their data. We prioritize transparency and minimal data collection to protect your privacy.

This app helps you manage your digital footprint with zero unnecessary data tracking. All features are built with privacy as the core principle.

Privacy Policy

1. Data We Collect

We collect only essential data to provide you with the best experience:

  • Device information (device type, OS version)
  • Usage patterns (feature interactions)
  • Consent preferences (privacy settings)
2. How We Use Your Data

Strictly for improving your experience and security:

  • Optimize app performance
  • Identify and fix security vulnerabilities
  • Provide you with privacy controls
3. Data Sharing

We never share your data with third parties without explicit consent. Data is processed only on your device.
