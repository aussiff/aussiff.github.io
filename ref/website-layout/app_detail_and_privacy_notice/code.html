About the Project

TaskMaster Pro started as a weekend hackathon project and evolved into a robust tool used by over 10,000 developers. The core philosophy is "distraction-free management." We stripped away the clutter found in enterprise tools like Jira and focused purely on what engineers need: speed, keyboard shortcuts, and deep integration with the tools they already use.

Built with a "offline-first" architecture, the app ensures that you can continue managing your backlog even when the internet cuts out, syncing seamlessly once connection is restored.

Privacy Notice

1. Data Collection

We believe in privacy by default. TaskMaster Pro does not collect personally identifiable information (PII) unless explicitly provided by you for account creation (email address). We do not sell your data to third parties.

2. Usage Data & Analytics

To improve app stability, we collect anonymous crash reports and aggregate usage metrics (e.g., "feature usage count"). This data cannot be traced back to individual users. You can opt-out of analytics in the App Settings.

3. Third-Party Services

We utilize trusted third-party services for specific functionalities:

  • Firebase Auth: For secure user authentication.
  • AWS S3: For storing user-uploaded attachments encrypted at rest.
  • Stripe: For processing subscription payments securely.

4. Contact Us

If you have questions regarding this privacy policy or your data rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@taskmasterpro.com.
