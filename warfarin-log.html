About Warfarin Log

WarfarinLog is a Clinical Decision Support (CDS) tool designed specifically for medical practitioners under the Ministry of Health (MOH) Malaysia, as well as allied healthcare professionals who manage oral anticoagulation therapy. The application assists in estimating Warfarin dosage adjustments based on patient International Normalised Ratio (INR) inputs, relying strictly on standardized local clinical guidelines.

Key Features

  • Daily Dosage Tracking: Record your daily Warfarin intake with ease
  • INR Monitoring: Log your INR (International Normalized Ratio) test results
  • Dosage Estimates: Get personalized dosage recommendations based on your history
  • Medication Reminders: Never miss a dose with customizable notifications
  • Historical Records: View your complete medication history and trends
  • Export Reports: Generate reports to share with your healthcare provider

Who Should Use This App?

This application is intended for:

  • Patients prescribed Warfarin for anticoagulation therapy
  • Healthcare providers monitoring patient Warfarin therapy
  • Caregivers helping manage medication for family members

Medical Disclaimer: This app is for informational and tracking purposes only. Always consult with your healthcare provider before making any changes to your medication dosage. The estimates provided by this app should not replace professional medical advice.
